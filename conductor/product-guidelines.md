# Product Guidelines - Hapback

## Tone and Voice
*   **Precise & Engineering-led:** The voice of Hapback is one of technical accuracy and clarity, providing a focused music listening experience with thoughtful interface design.
*   **Intuitive Labels:** We use intuitive labels and navigation structures that are familiar to music listeners (e.g., "Songs," "Artists," "Settings"). This ensures the user's mental model remains valid.

## UI/UX Principles
*   **Modern Minimalism:** The interface emphasizes clean, responsive design with subtle visual hierarchy. Meticulous attention to typography, spacing, and color creates an elegant user experience.
*   **Haptic-First Interaction:** Design is centered around the rotary control interface. Every visual transition and menu scroll should be a direct consequence of the control's "rotation" and "selections."
*   **Responsive Design:** The UI is developed with responsive principles to ensure high-fidelity presentation and a streamlined interaction model.

## Typography
*   **Legible Modern Typography:** Typography should be legible and provide excellent readability, prioritizing clarity and appropriate contrast.
*   **Clean Readability:** Legibility is key, with adequate contrast ratios and modern typeface selections.

## Interaction Design
*   **Feedback is Essential:** Interaction is enhanced with auditory and haptic feedback. Tactile responses confirm the user's actions.
*   **Fluid Motion:** Menu scrolling and content navigation should exhibit fluid motion and responsiveness to user input, providing a satisfying navigation experience.
