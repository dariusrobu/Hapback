# Product Definition - Hapback

## Initial Concept
Hapback is a native iOS/macOS application built using Swift and SwiftUI.

## Status
The foundational iPod Classic (6th Gen) user interface is implemented, including a functional virtual click wheel with haptic and auditory feedback. The Classic Home Page, Playlists, and Artists views are now fully functional with system library integration.

## Vision and Purpose
Hapback is a dedicated audio player designed to transport users back to the golden era of portable music. It is built for individuals who miss the specific, tactile feeling of owning and using a classic iPod. The goal is to recreate not just the look, but the soul of the device—the physical connection between the user and their music library.

## Target Audience
The primary users are music enthusiasts and tech nostalgists who crave the focused, physical experience of a dedicated music player. This includes anyone who wants to rediscover the joy of browsing a music collection through a mechanical-feeling interface rather than a modern touch-first design.

## Core Experience & Features
*   **Tactile Click Wheel:** A virtual click wheel that utilizes high-precision haptics to mimic the physical resistance and rotation of the original hardware.
*   **Authentic Auditory Feedback:** The interface is brought to life with authentic audio samples recorded from original iPod hardware (Classic, Mini, Video) for every click and selection.
*   **Cover Flow Navigation:** The legendary "Cover Flow" interface is reimagined for modern displays, allowing users to flick through their album artwork with fluid, physics-based motion.
*   **Music Integration:** Seamlessly connects with the local iOS music library and provides robust integration with Spotify and Apple Music APIs to ensure users can enjoy their entire modern collection through a classic lens.
*   **Mechanical Responsiveness:** The UI prioritizes the "momentum" and "inertia" characteristic of the original click-wheel menus, ensuring the software feels like a physical extension of the user's hand.

## Visual Aesthetic
Hapback adopts the iconic design language of the 6th Generation iPod Video/Classic. This includes the detailed, glossy UI elements, high-contrast typography (Myriad), and the specific layout patterns that defined the peak of the click-wheel era.
